3. Data Collected

📷 Photos and Images

The application accesses the device camera solely to enable receipt scanning. Images are processed locally on the device to extract discount information and are never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

📍 Location Data (GPS)

The application accesses geographic location exclusively to send proximity notifications when the user is near a store associated with an active discount. Location is never shared with third parties or used for profiling.

The user may revoke location permission at any time from the device settings.

4. Purposes of Processing

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Scanning and reading receipts to extract discount information
  • Sending reminder notifications before voucher expiry
  • Proximity notifications when near a store with an active discount

5. Protection of Minors

⚠️ The application may be used by users of any age, including minors. We adopt specific measures to ensure their protection.

  • We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable data from minors
  • Location data and images are never shared with third parties
  • Parents or guardians may request deletion of their child's data by contacting us
  • For children under 13, we recommend use under adult supervision

6. Legal Basis

Processing is based on the explicit consent of the user, expressed when the app is first opened and permissions are granted. Consent may be withdrawn at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

7. Data Retention

All data is stored exclusively on the user's device. We do not use external servers or cloud storage. Data is deleted automatically upon uninstallation of the app or manually by the user.

8. Third-Party Sharing

✅ The application does not share any data with third parties. We do not use analytics services, advertising, or external tracking tools.
