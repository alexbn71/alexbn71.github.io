❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a coupon?
Go to the Scan tab, point the camera at the coupon and press the shutter button. The AI automatically detects the store, amount and expiry date. You can also import an image from your gallery.

I'm not receiving proximity notifications. What should I do?
Make sure you have granted "Always" location permission in your system settings (Settings → Dealert → Location → Always). Also check that notifications are enabled.

Can I use Dealert without a camera?
Yes. You can import images from your gallery or enter data manually using the "Manual entry" button in the Scan tab.

Is my data safe?
All data (coupons, images, locations) is stored exclusively on your device. We do not use external servers. For details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete all my data?
Uninstall the app: all data is automatically removed from your device.

What is Dealert Premium?
The free plan allows you to save up to a limited number of active coupons. Premium removes this limit. You can subscribe from the Settings tab.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?
On iPhone: Settings → Your name → Subscriptions → Dealert → Cancel subscription.
